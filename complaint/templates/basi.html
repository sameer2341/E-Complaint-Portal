About Us

E-Complaint Portal is Platform For Students Who Face Issues In LGU and Want To Register Them .

Lahore Garrison universities do not provide any facility for students that make them feel free for complaining about their issues they are facing in their university. Sometimes some issues are beyond tolerance, which needs to be reach to authorities, which may even be helpful in the betterment of university. So, for that purpose our web application is very useful and it provide ease, comfort, freely and friendly way to complain about their issues

  • It will provide ease to students to register their complaints from anywhere or anytime.
  • It will help student to speak freely about their issues without any fear.
  • The Complaint Can either be General or Harassed
  • You Can Track Your complain

Our Purpose:

In Lahore Garrison university students face difficulties in solving their issues they do not even get a chance to speak about their complaints that they are facing in our daily routine. Complaints can be about any Area of University like regarding cafe, faculty, exam, accounts etc E-Complaint Portal is an online complaint portal for the students of Lahore Garrison University where they can post their complaint.

Betterment of University

E-Complaint Portal will help the University To Enhance the Quality of Their University WHich Lead them to Betterment.

Minimize The Faculity Fear.

E-Complaint Portal will minimize the usage of power of faculty/staff in wrong way.

Check And Balance

E-Complaint Portal help students to have check and Balance each of their complaints.
